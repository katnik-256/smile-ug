Who we are

Village of Hope Uganda (VOH) is a non-profit organization based in Uganda, we are dedicated in providing a better life through education and helping the needy

We are a dynamic and innovative team dedicated to providing exceptional assistance all across the country through supporting those in needy, educating the children, providing the necessities regardless of the color religion physical appearance and tribe.

We hope that working together could be one of strongest weapon we got in you continue support us by all means such that we support humanity together

Mission

Provide a safe, nurturing environment for orphaned children, promoting their physical, emotional, and educational well-being.

Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in our industry, recognized for our innovative approach, dedication, and contribution to sustainable growth.

Objectives

  • Provide shelter,food,clothing,and healthcare.
  • Ensure access to quality education.
  • Foster emotional support and psychological well-being.
  • Develop life skills and independence.
  • Facilitate social integration and community engagement.
